Online doctor benefits

Online doctor visits are more convenient, comfortable, and accessible than traditional in-person visits. Learn just some of the ways an online doctor can make your life easier, offering many benefits and providing top quality healthcare that works around you.

Accessible healthcare, wherever you are

With an online doctor, you can access medical care anywhere with a stable Internet connection. Being able to talk to a doctor online on your phone or computer means that you have more choices for care and treatment, especially if you are in a remote area or not currently near a medical facility or urgent care center.

Convenient care that fits your schedule

One of the advantages of virtual doctors is that online doctor appointments offer greater convenience and scheduling flexibility. A telehealth appointment means no more waiting rooms or time-consuming commutes. We often have same-day online medical consultations available, allowing you to find a time that works for your busy schedule.

Faster prescriptions, refills, and referrals

During your online doctor telemedicine visit, you can request prescriptions, refills, and referrals right when you need them, conveniently, from your phone or computer. During an online medical consultation with one of our licensed, board-certified doctors, they will review your medical history and work with you to discuss treatment options.

Timely, accessible preventive and ongoing care

The convenience and accessibility of online care makes it easy to take advantage of preventive care and address symptoms and concerns before they become larger health issues. Simply meet with your doctor online whenever and wherever it is convenient for you to access preventive care benefits, often free of cost with health insurance, saving you a trip to the doctor’s office.

High quality doctors

Telehealth provides patients with greater access to high quality care and treatment from highly trained medical professionals. We are proud to offer virtual primary care from the nation’s top doctors. All of our doctors have graduated from the top 50 U.S. medical schools.

Online doctor FAQs

The cost of an online doctor will vary depending on the telehealth platform and your insurance status. We accept patients with or without insurance. Most patients with in-network insurance pay just $30 to speak with one of our physicians. For self-pay patients, an online doctor appointment costs $129, and follow-ups are just $69. This is less than the typical cost at an in-person clinic.

You can see a doctor online by using a telehealth platform like ours. Our world-class doctors can answer your medical questions, make a professional diagnosis, and prescribe medications by phone or computer after an online medical consultation. Our licensed doctors can treat patients in all 50 states, are available 24/7, 360 days a year, and are all graduates from the top 50 U.S. medical schools.

Yes, an online doctor can diagnose you. Online doctors work great for treating non-emergency conditions such as minor bacterial infections, the cold and flu, and many mental health conditions. They are also useful for managing chronic conditions and getting prescriptions filled and refilled, without having to go to a doctor’s office in person.

Whether or not you need health insurance to see an online doctor will depend on the telehealth platform. We accept patients with or without health insurance. If you do not have in-network health insurance, you can still book an appointment with one of our board certified online doctors and receive treatment. For self-pay patients, we charge a fee of $129 for new patient appointments and $69 for follow-ups.

You need to have a consultation with a medical doctor before you can receive prescription medication. This can be done through an online doctor appointment via phone or video chat. If the doctor decides that you may benefit from a prescription, they will electronically send one to the pharmacy of your choice. Our online doctors can write prescriptions and prescription refills and will electronically send your prescription to the pharmacy of your choice, following your online appointment. They can also provide you with a doctor’s note, if necessary.
